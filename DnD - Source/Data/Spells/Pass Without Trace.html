Pass Without Trace: (2nd-Level Abjuration)
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Self
Components: V, S, M
  • V - You must be able to speak.
  • S - You must have at least one free hand.
  • M - You must have ashes from a burned leaf of mistletoe and a sprig of spruce.
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour

A veil of shadows and silence radiates from you, masking you and your companions from detection. For the duration, each creature you choose within 30 feet of you (including you) has a +10 bonus to Dexterity (Stealth) checks and can't be tracked except by magical means. A creature that receives this bonus leaves behind no tracks or other traces of its passage.

Player's Handbook (p.264)
