Jump: (1st-Level Transmutation)
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Touch
Components: V, S, M
  • V - You must be able to speak.
  • S - You must have at least one free hand.
  • M - You must have a grasshopper's hind leg.
Duration: 1 minute

You touch a creature. The creature's jump distance is tripled until the spell ends.

Player's Handbook (p.254)
